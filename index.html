--- layout: default title: GAAD celebration in Ottawa on May 16th, 2019! ---

Celebrate the 2019 edition of the Global Accessibility Awareness Day in Ottawa on Thursday May 16th, 2019 at Carleton University.

This year, talks and presentations will be about the Canada proposed Accessibility Act. We will have 2 keynotes speakers, 4 breakout sessions and a panel.

Join us at this free event to hear from experts about the proposed bill C81 and learn about accessibility from various perspectives such as Employment, Transportation, IT and Communication and Program Service Delivery.
