We are planning a special event to celebrate the 5th edition of the Global Accessibility Awareness Day in Ottawa.

The 3rd edition of a11yYOW will be held May 19th, 2016 at 1125@carleton (Human Computer Interaction Building (HCI) at Carleton University).

This event is about bringing together the web accessibility community to share ideas, best practices, experiences and hopefully to raise the bar when it comes to digital inclusion. If you're interested in accessibility & inclusive design then we want you at this event.

Global Accessibility Awareness Day Background

About Accessibility Camps

Accessibility Camp Ottawa takes its inspiration from the first Accessibility Camp DC, which was held in October 2009. Sister events include:

  • Accessibility Camp Toronto
  • Accessibility Camp Montreal
  • Accessibility Camp Quebec
  • Accessibility Camp Los Angeles
  • Boston Accessibility Group
  • A11y Camp Guelph
  • Accessibility Camp Seattle
