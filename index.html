Ottawa Accessibility Unconference

Saturday, December 6th 2014 from 8:30 AM until 5:00 PM

At Carleton University

Join us for a day of informal discussions, demos & debates concerning access to artifacts in our digital age.
Admission is free & everyone is invited.

Call for Proposals

On the day of the unconference all the attendees will vote on the speaking proposals we have received to elect eight speakers. This description will be posted on our website.

On the day of the unconference all the attendees will vote on the speaking proposals we have received to elect eight speakers. This description will be posted on our website.

Send us a message with the following details to make your proposal known:

  • Presentation Title
    Maximum 12 words. Unlimited images.
  • Who is the Audience?
    Is your presentation suitable for beginners, experienced practitioners or experts in the topic? Are there explicit lyrics?
  • Abstract
    Describe your presentation in 70 words or less. Do include hyperlinks to anything that might help to explain your intentions.
  • Biography
    Introduce yourself in 100 words or less. You may want to include your Twitter handle or website address.

Remember, you won’t be confirmed as a speaker until 10:00 AM on Saturday. Participants will be voting on all the proposals at the beginning of the day.
