Ottawa Accessibility Unconference

Saturday, December 6th 2014 from 8:30 AM until 5:00 PM

At Carleton University

Join us for a day of informal discussions, demonstrations & debates about access to artifacts our digital age.
Admission is free & everyone is invited.
